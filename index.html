This game is a roguelike survival game, where you will choose cards to progress through the levels. All the enemies and bosses are all mythical creatures from different fokelores and mythology. It is planned to be released at somepoint in June.

Upon dying, you have to restart your runs, so you have to learn how to tackle each enemy, and survive the bosses. So strategy is slighly required, as choosing cards directly affects the next level and possibly future levels if you get unlucky.

So play and enjoy my game.
